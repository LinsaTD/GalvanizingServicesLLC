    WITH BRITISH STANDARD

    BS EN ISO 1461:2009 & ASTM A 123/ A153
    The company has set up well-documented acceptance standards and testing criteria for the galvanized products, which are based on international standards For Hot dip galvanization on fabricated Iron and steel articles (BS EN ISO: 1461:2009, ASTM A 123, ASTM A 153 and ASTM A143M for hot dip Galvanizing. The acceptance criteria are strictly followed and any material, which deviates from the same, is rejected and reprocessed. It has been our continuous endeavor to improve our overall Quality Management System and our process system. GSE has followed a policy of continual improvement both in process as well as products. This has resulted in improved quality and speedy delivery of all our products thereby resulting in better customer satisfaction and we are sure to extend the same to our Galvanizing process. The above standards for the Hot dip Galvanized product give the requirement for coating weight, adhesion, appearance and uniformity.
