    Working Profile for Painting

    We offer a full range of surface preparation and protective coatings as follows:

    Blasting & Painting

    Clients: Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Power, Industrial & Construction
    Services: Abrasive blasting, Coating, and Painting of steel items.

    High Pressure Water Jetting

    Used for cleaning and degreasing operations efficiently.

    Conventional Grit Blasting

    Uses a range of abrasives to achieve high-quality surface preparation with a tailored profile to suit coating specifications.

    Wet Abrasive Blasting

    Ideal for selective coating removal and reducing airborne dust where grit blasting is impractical.

    Paint Stripping

    Used in sensitive areas (e.g. turbines, motors) with chemicals instead of blasting. Surfaces are cleaned after to prepare for coating.

    Power Tool Cleaning

    Surface preparation with tools like needle guns and grinders, suitable for areas where blasting is cost-prohibitive.

    Paint Application

    The aim is to apply a continuous, highly adherent film of even thickness. Key factors include coating type, weather, and method.

    Airless Spray Paint Coating

    Used for applying a complete range of industrial coating systems.
